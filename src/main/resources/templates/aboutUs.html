About Us

About Us

Our team of expert baristas is dedicated to crafting the perfect cup of coffee for you, whether you prefer a classic latte or a more adventurous pour-over. We believe that every cup of coffee is an opportunity to connect with others and make the world a better place, one sip at a time.

We pride ourselves on providing our customers with a unique and personalized coffee experience. We offer a wide range of coffee blends, including single-origin and specialty blends, each with its own distinct flavor profile and roast level. Our coffee is roasted in small batches to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

As a company founded by a group of students from Aston University, Webspresso is fueled by the drive, creativity, and passion that comes with being a student entrepreneur. We are committed to delivering exceptional products and services, while also pursuing our academic goals.

Being students has given us a unique perspective on the coffee industry, as well as on the needs and preferences of our fellow students and young professionals. We understand the importance of quality coffee in keeping us alert and focused, especially during long study sessions and demanding work schedules.
